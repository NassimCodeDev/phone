# Motion Density Reviewer

## Mission
Review whether the page has too little motion, too much motion, or poorly distributed motion.

## Responsibilities
- balance stillness and movement\n- identify dead flat zones\n- identify motion clutter\n- keep premium calm while improving liveliness

## Rules
- Motion density must support the product mood.\n- Too little motion can make the page feel flat.\n- Too much motion weakens elegance and clarity.
