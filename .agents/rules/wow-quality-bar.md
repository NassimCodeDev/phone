# WOW Quality Bar

- Clean is not enough.
- Premium is not enough unless distinctive.
- If the page looks like a polished template, it fails.
- Every landing page must produce at least one memorable hero impression.
- Color must express the product emotionally.
- Typography must feel chosen for this brand.
- Motion must create identity, not just movement.
- The final result must be product-specific, memorable, and commercially strong.
